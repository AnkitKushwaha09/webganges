Welcome to WebGanges Edutech

WebGanges Technologies Pvt. Ltd. conceived as computer education & technology promotion. We are representing “Microsoft Learning” from WebGanges Technology Pvt Ltd. WebGanges Technologies provides services in 2 domains, Development( Website Designing, Software & Apps.) and Education (Corporate & Students).

Education:
Company provides specialized training on Different technologies. We not only provide training but we groom a student into a professional & certify as Microsoft Certified Professional. WebGanges Technologies has a very committed team consisting of Microsoft Certified trainers who are continuously guiding, mentoring, counseling and coaching the students by providing them with exclusive personalized attention, which helps them to develop solid industry oriented knowledge.
