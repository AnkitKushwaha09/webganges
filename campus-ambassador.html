Campus Ambassdor Program

We're hiring students to work part-time with WebGanges on campus and help students do well in their courses. You get work experience, and we help more students succeed. It's a win-win!

What is the WebGanges Campus Ambassador Program?
The Webganges Campus Ambassador Program is a highly selective and proud Student Ambassador program where you become the face of Webganges in your campus. This is the program where you can express your passion for marketing and expand your professional network.

As a WebGanges Campus Ambassador, you play a vital role in providing student support to your campus community. You work side-by-side with faculty, students, and WebGanges professionals to help your peers succeed in school and provide valuable feedback on our products and services.

You are provided training and professional development, leadership and management opportunities. What's not to like? Join the team today!

Who is a Campus Ambassador?
Any student who is committed to helping us spread the words about Webganges through active marketing campaigns and ensure participation from his or her college.Our Campus Ambassador are passionate, well connected and creative.Campus Ambassador is a leader with a keen interest in starting and building a community.

Who is Eligible?

  • You have to be a college student (CSE,IT,ECE,EIE).
  • You are looking for a medium to explore your marketing ideas.
  • You are insanely curious about everything and you have that mad passion to learn and grow.
  • You’re a creative leader.


Responsibilities of a Campus Ambassador:

  • Create events, activities, and opportunities that help students.
  • Collect campus feedback through focus groups, surveys, and individual interviews.
  • Work with professionals at WebGanges to promote current products and shape future ones.


The Campus Ambassador will be expected to promote the company's brand in his/her institution via direct student connect, social media, posters etc. The campus ambassador will also be required to organize talk/seminars/workshops by taking permission of the institution authorities.

Come up with innovative ideas on how Webganges associate with your college.

What do you get for being a Campus Ambassdor?
Being Campus Ambassador is the first professional entry on your resume. It helps you develop leadership,communication and project management skills.


Rewards:

  • An official Certificate validating your work as WebGanges Campus Ambassador.
  • Time to time opportunities to win goodies like T-shirts, Books and much more.
  • Recognition in the form of name integration on our website.
  • Special awards and rewards based on the performance.
  • Performance linked financial incentives.
  • 35% Discounts on our Trainings Program.
  • Certificate and letter of recommendation on successful completion of the program.
  • Access to amazing goodies.
  • Opportunity to join us full-time after college.


Duration:
The program will start the moment you are selected and it will be a 6 month commitment. Since it is a performance based model, you decide the time you want to give. We would ideally like you to spend about 4-5 hours a week.
